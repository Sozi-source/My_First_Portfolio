Healthy Eating

A healthy diet emphasizes foods that are rich in essential nutrients such as vitamins, minerals, protein, healthy fats, and fiber. These include:

  • Fruits and vegetables: Prioritize a variety of colorful, seasonal, and locally grown produce.
  • Whole grains: Opt for unprocessed grains like oats, brown rice, millet, and whole wheat.
  • Protein sources: Include plant-based options like beans, lentils, nuts, and seeds, alongside moderate amounts of animal proteins like fish, eggs, and lean meats.
