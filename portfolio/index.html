Designing the School Feeding Program

This project aims to combat hunger and malnutrition among schoolchildren in Wajir County, a region facing food insecurity challenges. I am designing a sustainable school feeding program that ensures access to balanced, nutritious meals. The program prioritizes locally available resources to promote community involvement and economic growth while improving children’s attendance, concentration, and academic performance. Designing the Toto Afya Program

The Toto Afya Program focuses on improving child health and nutrition in Nairobi’s underserved slum communities. I am leading the development of a holistic initiative that includes regular health checkups, nutrition education for caregivers, and the provision of nutrient-dense meals for children. The program also incorporates community workshops to raise awareness about child health, aiming to empower parents and guardians with practical knowledge to improve their families' well-being.
